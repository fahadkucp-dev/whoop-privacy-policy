Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

Overview

This privacy policy describes how this application ("the App") collects, uses, and protects data accessed through the WHOOP API. This App is a personal health tracking tool built on the WHOOP Developer Platform.

Data We Access

With your explicit authorization via WHOOP's OAuth flow, the App may access the following data from your WHOOP account:

  • Recovery data — recovery score, heart rate variability (HRV), and resting heart rate
  • Sleep data — sleep duration, start and end times, and sleep performance metrics
  • Cycle data — daily strain scores and average heart rate during a cycle
  • Heart rate data — real-time and historical heart rate readings

We only request access to data that is necessary for the App's core functionality.

How We Use Your Data

Your WHOOP data is used solely to provide the features of this App — such as displaying your health metrics, generating trends, or storing your personal health history. Your data is never sold, rented, or shared with any third party for advertising, marketing, or commercial purposes.

Data Storage and Security

Any data retrieved from the WHOOP API may be stored locally on your device or in a personal database under your control. We take reasonable technical precautions to protect your data, including secure storage of access tokens and encrypted connections. Access tokens are refreshed regularly and stored securely.

Data Sharing

We do not share your personal health data with any third parties. Your data is used only for your own personal use within the App.

Your Rights and Controls

You can revoke this App's access to your WHOOP data at any time through your WHOOP account settings. Upon revocation, the App will no longer be able to retrieve new data. You may request deletion of any locally stored data at any time.

Third-Party Services

This App uses the WHOOP API to access your health data. Your use of WHOOP's platform is subject to WHOOP's own Privacy Policy. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by WHOOP.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how your data is handled, please contact the app owner.
